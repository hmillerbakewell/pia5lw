Guess the word! You get seven tries. The computer chooses from a list of five letter dictionary words. The words are root words, meaning no plurals or conjugations, but can include scientific terms.

  • Green background: Right letter, right place.
  • Blue background: The letter appears somewhere else in the word.
  • Grey background: The letter does not appear in the word.
  • Grey word: The word is not in the word list (e.g. plurals), so this doesn't count as a guess.

If the word isn't in the dictionary then the response will be shown greyed out, and it won't cost you a guess.

The source word list is a modification of the common word list from the Moby project. It contains many words that I, the author, had not encountered before. Good luck.
